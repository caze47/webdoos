Our Philosophy

At DuBo, We Believe In More Than Just Training. We Cultivate Strength, Discipline, And Respect In Every Student Through Physical And Mental Fortitude.

Dobu was founded in 2010 by Mauricio Gomez. Since then, our dojo has grown from a small one to one of the premier martial arts training centers in the United Kingdom. We focus ourselves on creating a welcoming environment where students of all ages and skill levels can prosper.
